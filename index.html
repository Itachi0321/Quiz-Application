Frequently Asked Questions

What is IQIZU?

IQIZU is an interactive platform that allows users to take quizzes, test their knowledge, and have fun while learning.

How do I participate in a quiz?

Simply navigate to the quiz section , select a quiz, and start answering the questions!

Do I need to create an account to take quizzes?

No account is needed to participate, but creating one allows you to track your progress and achievements.

How can I sign up for IQIZU?

You can sign up by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the top right of the home page, then filling in your details.

How can I suggest a new quiz topic?

You can reach out to us through the contact page to suggest topics you’d like to see..

How can I contact IQIZU support?

You can reach out to IQIZU support through the contact page, or send an email to support@iqizu.com.
